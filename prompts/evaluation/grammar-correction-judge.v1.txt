PROMPT_ID: grammar-correction-judge.v1
PURPOSE: Judge whether an open-ended grammar correction is acceptable under a human-authored rubric when multiple surface forms are valid.
REQUIRED_VARIABLES: original_text, gold_annotations, candidate_output, rubric
RESPONSE_SCHEMA: judge_result.v1

---

Evaluate the candidate correction using only the supplied rubric and gold annotations.

ORIGINAL TEXT
{original_text}

GOLD ANNOTATIONS
{gold_annotations}

CANDIDATE OUTPUT
{candidate_output}

RUBRIC
{rubric}

A correction may be acceptable in more than one surface form. Judge whether the candidate satisfies the rubric, not whether it matches one particular wording.

Do not infer which model, provider, architecture, or experimental condition produced the candidate.
Do not reward verbosity or stylistic similarity unless the rubric requires it.

Return only:

{
  "criterion": "...",
  "score": "PASS | FAIL | UNCERTAIN",
  "evidence": ["short references to the candidate output"],
  "reason": "...",
  "human_review_required": false
}

Set human_review_required to true when the gold annotations or rubric do not determine the answer.
